REVIEW

Johsua Lopez April 22, 2026

A Silent Voice is one of my favorite movies because it feels emotional and realistic. What stands out most to me is how it shows guilt, loneliness, and the struggle to make things right after hurting someone. The story is not just sad, though. It is also about growth, forgiveness, and learning how to understand other people.

I also like how calm and beautiful the movie looks. The quieter scenes make the emotions feel stronger, and the characters feel very human. Shoya and Shoko both go through a lot, which makes the movie more meaningful to watch. Overall, I think A Silent Voice is powerful, memorable, and one of the best anime films I have seen.
